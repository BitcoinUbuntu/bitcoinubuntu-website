PoWBoT & the CBAF

We built PoWBoT – a Telegram bot that verifies and rewards Proof of Work for the Circular Bitcoin Africa Fund.

What is PoWBoT?

A bot that rewards real-world Bitcoin adoption. Projects submit videos of Bitcoin payments at local merchants. If approved, sats flow to the merchant, the project, and the reviewer via Lightning.

What is the CBAF?

The Circular Bitcoin Africa Fund (CBAF) – FBCE's dedicated program supporting 42+ Bitcoin Circular Economy projects across Africa, backed by Bitcoin Beach.

Our Role

Bitcoin Ubuntu built and maintains PoWBoT. We also provide technical support to all 42+ CBAF member projects and contribute our skills to the broader mission of Bitcoin adoption in Africa.

About Bitcoin Ubuntu

Ubuntu: "I am because we are" – a philosophy of community, shared humanity, and mutual support.

Our Mission

To make free, high-quality Bitcoin education accessible to everyone in Africa. We believe financial sovereignty is a human right, and Bitcoin is the tool to achieve it.

Who We Are

A volunteer organization based in Swellendam, South Africa. Working to orange-pill the continent one community at a time.

Open Source

Everything we publish is freely available. Our curriculum, slides, tutorials, and tools are open for anyone to use, modify, and share.
